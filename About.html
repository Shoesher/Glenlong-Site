About

Richard Ma is the author of the Glen Long series. Currently 15,
he started writing in middle school when he and a few of his friends went to Yorkdale Mall
and got their friend Emmanuel a Jojo's t-shirt as a belated birthday gift.
When they went to give it to him he told them to meet up at "Glen Long Park" hence the name but
when he arrived on a scooter and started dueling Richard's friends Shahriar and Jake
with sticks, Richard found it funny and recorded it. These events are what inspired the original
the original Glen Long Saga.

The story was relatively popular in his friend group for a while, during this time he improved
his art and introduced new characters such as Han, Stephanie, William, and Yazen.
However, when he was about to end the series his friend Han gave him
some heavy criticisms about the original stories which Richard took into account.
Leading him to rewrite the entire story with significantly better art and writing.
That brings us to today currently where he has completed volumes 1 and 2 and the first part
of volume 3 of the rewrite. These new stories are considered his best works yet but that
isn't the end of it as he strives to improve the story even further with the new upcoming
second part of volume 3.
